Steven Ackx's Summary

I started my career in the Media/Movie-theatre sector, where I was involved in the introduction of new technologies, like Internet, e-ticketing and e-business. After this I joined the Ubizen (aka Netvision, Cybertrust and Verizon) company where I continued my career starting as a pre-sales consultant/engineer. Two years later I was promoted to technical product manager of two high-volume security products, which were developed in-house by Ubizen. As a technical product manager I managed a team of more than twenty persons, both commercially and technically skilled. After being a technical product manager at Ubizen for more than two years, I joined the Ascure company as an Information Security Consultant and Coach mainly working on Information Security Governance, Operational Risk Management and Security Management. Today I am also part of the management of Ascure, managing the business unit Ascure Academy and acting as COO (together with my team we are responsible for the day2day operations of Ascure). I have a consultancy and management background in networking and security applications disciples and have engaged in several major and medium security and related projects throughout my career. I have the following certifications EMBIS, CISM, CISSP and CISA. Recently I have been appointed as CEO of a new company, called BCM Academy Belgium (more information can be found on www.bcmacademy.be).

Specialties

I work as a BU Director and Operations Director for Ascure and I'm the CEO of BCM Academy BE. I'm also member of the "Strategic Services" BU as a Principal Operational Risk Management Consultant (project manager, info sec governance manager and risk management) for customers of Ascure. My main consultancy and coaching focuss lays in information security governance, operational risk management, education and awareness. I'm also responsible for the day2day operations of Ascure.
